WHAT IS BLISS?

Bliss, an IOS mobile application tool for dealing with stress and anxiety through meditation and de-stressing strategies. Use Bliss to bring peace into one’s life and experience the benefits of meditation and positive visualization.

Mental illness is so common in today's society, anyone could be experiencing it. The worst part - the ignorance of its prevalence in today's youth and adults.

Download

MISSION

Bliss’s mission is to help one’s mental health through meditations, positive visualization and meaningful actions. Especially in today’s society, it is important to emphasize on the significance in dealing with mental health.

Meditation

Relax your mind through meditation and deep breathing. Meditation reduces stress, enforces a healthy lifestyle, and increase self- awareness. In current society, high-pressure environments are all around us; with meditation, it may help control anxiety in those anxiety-filled environment. Over a long period of time, it will help maintain lower anxiety levels in those surroundings.

Actions

Set daily action challenges and goals in order to fulfill a positive and meaningful long-term life. The goal for this is to help build healthier relationships with others. Also, by accomplishing the daily action challenges, it will give users a little sense of accomplishment everyday.

Positive Visualization

Visualize one’s desired outcomes in order to help achieve one’s wanted long-term goals. Positive visualization improves one’s self- confidence, level of happiness, and creativity. Through visualization techniques, it will help visualize one's long-term desires and help create a way of accomplishing their goal. Through this activity, it will activate user's creative subconsciousness to create creative ways to achieve one's goal. Overall, positive visualization will structure one's internal motivation and take necessary actions in order to achieve one's goals.

FEATURES

Bliss the app has many different features that are meant to help deal with one's stress and allow one to be more calm in times of stress. By applying and practicing these features will improve one's relationship with others and with oneself. The Meditation and the positive visualization features build upon one's self-love and self-help. While actions feature build the one's relationship with others.
